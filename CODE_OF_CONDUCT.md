# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free project environment.

Participants should communicate professionally, focus criticism on technical work rather than individuals, respect differing experience levels, and avoid publishing private or confidential information. Maintainers may edit or remove abusive content and may restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers through the RAISE LAB contact information rather than in a public issue.
